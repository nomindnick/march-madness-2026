## Category Strategy

- **Chalk (1-2):** Safe picks. R2+ uses win probability favorites. Highest mean scores (~229 pts). Insurance against favorites winning.
- **Value (3-6):** 2-seed and 3-seed champions with strong odds. R2+ chalk with selective R1 diversification. Balanced risk/reward (~212-223 pts mean).
- **Contrarian (7-9):** Higher-seed champions (3-4 seeds) with EV-optimized R2+ picks. More upsets, lower mean scores (~196-210 pts) but much higher upside (90th percentile up to 294 pts). These pay off big when upsets happen.
- **Swing (10):** Maximum differentiation from other 9 brackets. EV R2+ with all close-call upsets. Covers scenarios where the other 9 miss.
